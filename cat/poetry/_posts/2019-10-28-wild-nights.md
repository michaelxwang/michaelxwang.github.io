四言版·狂野之夜<br>
作者：艾米莉·狄金森<br>

狂野之夜<br>
愿与君享<br>
良辰应是<br>
玉宇琼浆<br>

风儿无奈<br>
心已入港<br>
收起海图<br>
不再远航<br>

泛舟伊甸<br>
爱的海洋<br>
我愿今夜<br>
泊于君旁<br>

七绝版·夜之狂<br>
作者：艾米莉·狄金森<br>
麦克译<br>

雨夜无君妄自狂<br>
西风不入梦之乡<br>
泛舟伊甸情如海<br>
愿泊君心夜夜长<br>

Wild Nights<br>
By Emily Dickinson<br>

Wild Nights - Wild Nights<br>
Were I with thee<br>
Wild Nights should be<br>
Our luxury!<br>

Futile - the winds -<br>
To the heart in port -<br>
Done with the compass -<br>
Done with the chart!<br>

Rowing in Eden -<br>
Ah, the sea!<br>
Might I but moor Tonight -<br>
In Thee!<br>
