I have uploaded a "*_SIGN PDF file for you to sign, you can use one of the following alternative ways to sign.

  • Print the form, sign and date, scan, and upload the image.
  • Sign the PDF file on Apple computer following this instruction.
  • Sign the PDF file on Windows computer following this instruction.

There are two common ways to create a signature:

  • Traditional Handwritten Signature: Create your signature by hand using a pen on a physical medium such as a piece of paper.
  • Digital Handwritten Signature: Use a computer input device, such as a stylus or a mouse, to hand-draw your signature on a digital platform or application.
