The tax return workflow

The six-layer protocol has been tested by so many people over so many years.
If you agree to follow the protocol, then let us get started.

Existing clients start from step [2a].

  • → [1a] You provide me: ["Your Gmail address", "First name", "Last name"].
  • ← [1b] I will send you a *secure* link for you to upload your tax documents.

  • → [2a] You upload your tax documents according to the checklist.
  • ← [2b] I provide a fixed price based on low end market price minus a discount.

  • → [3a] You tell me that you agree with the price.
  • ← [3b] I prepare your tax return and upload to the same link for your verification.

  • → [4a] You tell me you have verified OK or you ask questions.
  • ← [4b] I send you an electronic signature request (or I upload a signature document).

  • → [5a] You tell me that you have signed.
  • ← [5b] I will submit your tax return electronically (or file on paper in rare cases).

  • → [6a] You pay me according to what we have agreed previously.
  • ← [6b] I acknowledge your payment.
